Inter-Provincial Lifetime Migration Proportion Matrix for Population by Province : Female

  • Belenga Dev

Interactive chart lifetime migration between Zimbabwe's provinces




The chart illustrates inter-provincial lifetime migration proportions for the population by province. Notable migration patterns include significant movements from Bulawayo to Harare and Midlands, as well as from Mashonaland Central to Harare. Conversely, Harare receives substantial migration inflows from various provinces. Overall, migration tends to be concentrated towards major urban centers like Harare and Midlands, indicating urbanization trends. Total migration stands at 14,932,940 individuals, with Harare having the highest total migration both in and out of the province.
